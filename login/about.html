PROFILE

OUR STORY

Welcome to Burger 4.0

Founded in 2015, Burger 4.0 began as a small food truck with a big dream - to serve the most delicious, juicy burgers in town. What started as a humble operation with just three burger varieties has now grown into a beloved local chain with five locations across the city.

Quality Ingredients

At Burger 4.0, we're committed to using only the freshest, highest quality ingredients. Our beef is 100% grass-fed, our buns are baked fresh daily by local artisans, and our vegetables are sourced from trusted regional farms.

We never use frozen patties or artificial preservatives. Each burger is made to order, ensuring you get the perfect burger every time. Our secret sauce? Made from a family recipe that's been passed down for generations.
